What is Covid-19 ?

Coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2 (SARS-CoV-2). Learn More

Origin of Covid-19 ?

Covid-19 was first identified in December 2019 in Wuhan, China. The first case may be traced back to 17 November 2019. Learn More

Causes of Covid-19 ?

Common Symptoms of Covid-19

The presence of Covid-19 can be identified through the following symptoms

  • Continous Cough and Sneezing
  • Shortness of breath
  • Fever
  • Fatigue

NB: Sometimes there may be no prevailing symptoms in the victim, but make sure you go for diagonosis

Common Preventive Measures

Since there is currently no active medication for Covid-19, we should try and follow the prevention guidlines

HAND WASHING

Wash your hand with soap and clean water or use sanitizer

COVER A COUGH OR SNEEZE

Use tissues or sleeves to cover your cough or sneeze

SOCIAL DISTANCING

Maintaining at least 2 metres (6 feet) physical distance between yourself and individuals

FACE COVERINGS

Cover your face with face covers and prevent spread of the disease

PARACTICING GOOD RESPIRATORY HYGIENE
